Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Guess My College. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, safeguard, and share information when you use our website, prediction tools, and 1:1 expert guidance services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you sign in securely via Google, we collect basic profile information such as your name, email address, and profile picture to sync your predictions across devices.
  • Academic Data: To calculate your score and predict your college, we process your MHT-CET response sheets, calculated raw marks, and your specific reservation category (e.g., GOPENS, GOBCS, EWS, SC, ST).
  • Transaction Data: If you book a 1:1 Expert Guidance Session, securely processed payment details are collected via our third-party payment gateways. We do not store your credit card or UPI details on our servers.
  • Communication Data: Phone numbers and chat logs if you contact us directly via phone or WhatsApp for support or session tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly utilized to provide you with the most accurate admission advantage:

  • To parse your correct/incorrect tally and calculate your exact raw MHT-CET score.
  • To map your marks against our algorithmic data (10,000+ past cutoffs) and generate highly accurate college predictions.
  • To build a customized, personalized CAP preference form during your booked sessions.
  • To manage your account, verify referral rewards ("Refer & Earn"), and provide customer support.

4. Data Security & Sharing

We implement industry-standard encryption to protect your response sheets and login tokens. We do not sell your personal or academic data to third parties, educational institutions, or marketing agencies. Your data is only shared with trusted service providers (such as secure cloud hosts and payment processors) solely for the purpose of operating our platform.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you, request corrections to any inaccurate data, or request full deletion of your account and uploaded response sheets. To exercise these rights, or to disconnect your Google account from our services, please contact us.
