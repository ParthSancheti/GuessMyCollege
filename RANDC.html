Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

1. Policy Overview

This policy governs the terms of cancellation and refunds specifically for our 1:1 Expert Guidance Sessions (priced at ₹899). As this service involves blocking dedicated time with our expert counselors and generating custom data models, strict timelines apply to cancellation requests.

2. Cancellation Timelines & Refunds

Time of Cancellation Refund Eligibility
More than 24 hours before the scheduled session 100% Full Refund
Less than 24 hours before the scheduled session No Refund (Free Rescheduling Allowed)
No-show / Missing the session without prior notice No Refund

3. Rescheduling Policy

If you cannot attend your scheduled session, you may request to reschedule it up to 6 hours before the meeting begins at no additional cost. Rescheduling is subject to counselor availability.

4. Cancellations by Guess My College

In the rare event of technical difficulties or counselor unavailability, Guess My College reserves the right to cancel or reschedule your session. If we cancel, you will be offered the choice of a prioritized rescheduled time slot or a 100% full refund.

5. Post-Session Refunds

Because the Expert Guidance Session involves immediate delivery of intellectual property (customized CAP preference forms based on algorithmic data) and dedicated human time, no refunds are issued once a session has been successfully completed. However, as per our guarantee, we offer continued mentorship via WhatsApp until your admission is secure.

6. Processing Timelines

Approved refunds will be credited back to your original payment method (e.g., Credit/Debit Card, UPI, or Net Banking) within 5 to 7 business days.
